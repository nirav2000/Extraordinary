Why this matters

Young High Flyers is built to surface stories worth studying, not just admiring.

Each profile highlights the achievement, the age, the context, and a practical insight on why it matters. Some entries are inspiring, some cautionary, and all are designed to spark research, writing, or curriculum ideas.
